video script: I fell into a shadow-drenched hall. A hundred doors, a hundred dead ends. Each lock a silent mockery, a stark black promise I couldn't reach. Then, a glint. On a glass table, a tiny golden key. It fit a door no bigger than my hand, revealing a vibrant, sun-drenched garden.

A bottle materialized, whispering "DRINK ME" in glowing crimson letters. I obeyed, my world warping and shrinking until I was smaller than a teacup. But the garden was still out of reach. A cake appeared, frosted with "EAT ME." One bite, and I shot upwards, a giant in a dollhouse.

Panic set in. I was a trapped titan. Hot, sharp tears fell like a torrential downpour, pooling and flooding the distorted checkerboard floor below. Then, I shrank once more, tumbling helplessly into the salty ocean I had just cried. Animals swam past me in the chaotic, swirling depths.

We scrambled ashore, a drenched and shivering menagerie on the edge of my own sorrow. A Mouse, a Dodo, a Duck, all looking lost. The Dodo declared a Caucus Race! A mad, circular dash with no start or finish. We all won and received odd, nonsensical prizes.

Then, a flicker of white. The Rabbit! He glanced back, his red eyes a challenge. I forgot everything else and plunged into the dark forest after him.

Style Design: The video will be rendered in a cell-shaded anime style heavily inspired by the video game "Killer is Dead." This is defined by a hyper-stylized, graphic aesthetic featuring thick, angular, and expressive black outlines on all characters and objects. The color palette is vibrant and saturated, applied in distinct, flat blocks with minimal blending. Shading is achieved with high-contrast, sharply defined geometric shadows, often in pure black, creating a dramatic and graphic look reminiscent of ink wash paintings. Light sources are strong and artificial, casting harsh highlights and deep shadows to enhance the dramatic, surreal atmosphere.

character design: Alice:

A young female in her late teens, lean and athletic.
Long, wavy golden blonde hair, tied back with a large red bow on top.
Large, expressive blue eyes accentuated with prominent black eyeliner and long lashes.
Fair skin with a light blush on her cheeks.
Wears a deep royal blue, puffed-sleeve dress with a white frilly apron over it. The apron is detailed with red-centered, heart-shaped cutouts.
Accessories include a red bow at her neck, white thigh-high stockings with red garters, and red Mary Jane shoes, each with a small bow.
White Rabbit:

An anthropomorphic rabbit with a black and white fur pattern.
Piercing red eyes with slit pupils.
Dressed in formal attire: a dark blue/black top hat with a lighter blue band, a royal blue tailcoat suit over a vest, and a white ruffled shirt.
Wears a blue bow tie and holds a golden pocket watch on a chain.
Features long, upright ears with pink interiors.
